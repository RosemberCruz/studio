# **App Name**: TramitesFacil

## Core Features:

- Service Directory: Browse a categorized directory of available government services and procedures.
- Procedure Guides: Step-by-step guides for completing each service or procedure, including required documents and forms.
- Form Generator Tool: AI powered tool to generate partially completed forms based on user inputs and intelligently incorporates official instructions.
- Appointment Scheduler: Schedule appointments with government agencies directly through the app.
- Document Checklist: Create and manage checklists of required documents for each procedure.
- Progress Tracker: Track the progress of your applications and procedures.
- Notifications & Alerts: Receive notifications about deadlines, appointment reminders, and application status updates.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#79A7D3), evoking trust and efficiency.
- Background color: Very light blue (#EBF4FA).
- Accent color: Warm orange (#D38F79), used sparingly for calls to action and important notifications.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that balances modernity and approachability.
- Use clean, simple icons to represent different services and procedures. Ensure icons are easily recognizable and consistent throughout the app.
- Design a clean, intuitive layout that prioritizes easy navigation and accessibility. Use clear headings, labels, and visual cues to guide users through the app.
- Incorporate subtle animations and transitions to enhance user experience and provide feedback. For example, use animations to confirm successful actions or highlight important information.
